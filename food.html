Kue Cucur

Cucur is a typical Betawi cake which is brownish in color. This Cucur cake also has a round shape with a thicker middle than the edges, and the edges themselves have a slightly dry texture.

Kue Rangi

The original Bekasi community is an urban Betawi and Sundanese community. Therefore, it is not surprising that most of Bekasi's typical food is Betawi style food. Likewise, with Rangi cake.

Kue Telur Gabus

In Bekasi, not only cork pucung is famous, but also its eggs even though they are not real eggs. Currently, this cork egg cake can be found in many souvenir shops everywhere, even in other cities.

Kue Sagon

Sagon cake is a typical Bekasi snack which is made from sticky rice flour. This rice flour can be roasted first as a basic ingredient. Ausually this sagon cake is mixed with a sprinkling of grated coconut on top.
