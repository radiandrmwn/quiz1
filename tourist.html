Alun-Alun Bekasi

Alun-Alun Bekasi has very adequate facilities for the community, especially after repairs were carried out in almost all corners of the square. Here you can find a lot of tenants that sells foods and drinks that stays up until midnight.

Summarecon Mall Bekasi

Summarecon Mall Bekasi is a great place to shop, you can find all the needs from fashion to home needs. Summarecon mall bekasi is one of the largest mall in the city of Bekasi There's also a theater to watch movies up on the third floor.

Rainbow Garden

Rainbow Garden Harapan Indah is a recreational park that is especially suitable for families. With an 'eat and play' concept, Rainbow Garden Bekasi combines a children's play area with culinary tourism. Various rides and activities are available at this colorful tourist park.
